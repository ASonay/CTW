Ideas (ctw_resources, 'IdeaState'):
	undiscovered
		Can be discovered by contacting NPCs
	discovered
		Has to be moved to team billboard
    published
		Published on the team billboard
		Members can start to collect resources (books, technologies, ideas)
	approved
		NPC approved the idea (brought resources)
	inventing
		WIP, see billboard status
	invented
		Team gains new technologies

Gameplay:
	Teams start at their base
	Each team has a shared Discovery Points (DP) value

Target:
	Get ideas
	Build wires to the data centre (middle)
	Transfer data to gain DPs
